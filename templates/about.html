What is SearchBuddy?

SearchBuddy helps you find songs based on lyrics. Just type in a line or phrase, and we’ll return the closest match—even if the lyrics are vague or incomplete.

It works best with popular songs, but we’re constantly improving accuracy for all genres and regions.

Note: This is an experimental tool. Some matches may be approximate or incorrect—especially for lesser-known tracks. Your feedback helps us improve!

What is SearchBuddy?

Ever had a lyric stuck in your head but couldn't remember the song? SearchBuddy helps you find songs based on lyrics. Just type in a line or phrase from any song, and we'll return the closest match using advanced search systems.

We intelligently guess the most likely track—even if the lyrics are vague or incomplete. It works best with popular songs, but we're constantly improving accuracy for all genres and regions.

Note: This is an experimental tool. Some matches may be approximate or incorrect—especially for lesser-known tracks. Your feedback helps us improve!
